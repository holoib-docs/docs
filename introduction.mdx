## What is Holoib?

Holoib is an interactive demo platform designed to help companies easily create and share engaging product demos with their prospects, customers, and all relevant people.

## Who is Holoib for?

Anyone who requires interactive demos can benefit from Holoib. This includes, but isn't limited to, product managers, sales teams, technical support teams, trainers, and educators. It is perfect for those who wish to improve the interactivity of their presentations and work demos.
